Experience You Can Trust

For over two decades, we've been the go-to dojo in Ottawa for martial arts enthusiasts. Our passion for teaching is only matched by our love for Arnis, the Filipino martial art that will make you feel like a warrior!

Our instructors are not just teachers; they are seasoned fighters who have battled their way through competitions and life.

Join us and discover the thrill of mastering Arnis while making friends and memories that will last a lifetime. Who knows, you might even impress your friends with your newfound skills!

FAQs

What is Arnis exactly?

Arnis is a Filipino martial art focusing on weapon-based fighting, especially with sticks!

Do I need prior experience?

Nope! All levels are welcome to join!

What should I wear to class?

Comfortable clothes and a warrior spirit!

How often are classes held?

Classes are held multiple times a week!

Can I try a class for free?

Absolutely! Your first class is on us!
